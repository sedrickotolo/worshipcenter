About Us

Worship Centre Ministries emerged in May 2014 during a transitional leadership gap in PAG Lira Pastorate where a section of leaders seceded from the main stream leadership leaving Many believers with a desire of an environment of True Worship, a place one can find healing.

Believers who were thirsty for a congregational worship teamed up to begin a service. In their midst were servants of God who have a passion for True Worship and a Leadership that transforms a Christian life through holistic approach.

Mathew 5:14-15.” you are the Light of World” Shine your Light so your Father in Heaven be glorified”

To date over 1,000 believers sit under a tent “The Worship Centre” celebrating the love of Jesus Christ on every Sunday congregational worship. And for this just cause hope has found its home within us Alternative Worship Centre”. Be one among the growing 400 members today!
